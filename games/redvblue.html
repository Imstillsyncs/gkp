Red VS Blue

Instructions and Controls

First player controls:

W key is to go up

A key is to go left

S key is to go down

D key is to go right

X is to shoot

Second player controls:

↑ key is to go up

↓ key is to go down

→ key is to go right

← key is to go left

N is to shoot

G key to mute

H key to unmute

The point of this game is to shoot the other player to get a point. First one to get to 30 points wins, player one is red and player two is blue, do your best and good luck...
